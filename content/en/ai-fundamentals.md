# AI Fundamentals

## What is Artificial Intelligence?

Artificial Intelligence (AI) is a branch of computer science focused on building systems that can perform tasks typically requiring human intelligence. These tasks include understanding natural language, recognizing images, making decisions, and generating creative content.

AI is not a single technology — it encompasses machine learning, natural language processing, computer vision, robotics, and more. The AI systems most people interact with today, like ChatGPT or voice assistants, are examples of "narrow AI" — systems designed for specific tasks.

## How AI Learns

Most modern AI systems use machine learning, where algorithms improve through experience rather than explicit programming. The process works like this: the system is fed large amounts of training data, it identifies patterns in that data, and it adjusts its internal parameters to make better predictions.

Deep learning, a powerful subset of machine learning, uses artificial neural networks with many layers. These networks can learn incredibly complex patterns — from recognizing faces in photos to understanding the nuances of human language.

## Large Language Models

Large Language Models (LLMs) are AI systems trained on massive text datasets to understand and generate human language. Models like GPT, Claude, and Gemini learn by predicting the next word in a sequence — a deceptively simple objective that, at scale, produces remarkably capable systems.

LLMs can write essays, answer questions, summarize documents, translate languages, and even write code. However, they have important limitations: they can generate confident-sounding but incorrect information ("hallucinations"), they reflect biases in their training data, and they lack true understanding.

## Using AI Responsibly

As AI becomes more powerful, using it responsibly becomes crucial. Key principles include:
- **Verify AI outputs** — Always fact-check important information
- **Protect privacy** — Don't share sensitive personal data with AI systems
- **Acknowledge AI assistance** — Be transparent when AI helped create your work
- **Understand limitations** — AI is a tool, not an infallible oracle
- **Consider bias** — AI systems can perpetuate and amplify existing societal biases
