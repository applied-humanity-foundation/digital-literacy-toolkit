🌎 Our Mission

About This Project

The Digital Literacy Toolkit is part of Applied Humanity Foundation's commitment to making technology accessible and understandable for everyone, regardless of background or experience level.

We believe that digital literacy is a fundamental skill in the modern world. Access to clear, free, and interactive educational resources should not be limited by geography, income, or circumstance.

Every module is crafted by educators and reviewed by the community. All content is licensed under Creative Commons, ensuring it remains free for individuals, schools, libraries, and organizations everywhere.
